Image Editing
=============

Great images can bring life to your text. To simplify the incorporation of better images into your content, Brightspot contains a streamlined but powerful selection of image editing tools. All of Brightspot's image editing tools are completely nondestructive, so you can experiment as much as you like without changing the original.
